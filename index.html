Super Easy Rosemary Bread

Ingredients

  • 1 cup warm water
  • 2½ teaspoons acyive dry yeast
  • 3 tablespoons white sugar
  • 1½ teaspoon salt
  • 3 tablespoons olive oil
  • ½ teaspoon ground thyme
  • ½ teaspoon garlic powder
  • 2 teaspoons crushed driend rosemary
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour

Kitchen Equipment

  • Bread Machine
  • Measuring cups and spoons

Directions

  1. Pour water into pan of bread machine, then add yeast and sugar.
  2. (let sit until creamy foam forms on top of water: about 10 minutes).
  3. Sprinkle in seasonings and flour.
  4. Set bread machine to 1-1½ lb loaf. Set bread crust to light medium.
  5. Start Machine.
